King's Ascend 隱私條款
最後更新日期：2025 年 11 月 8 日

歡迎使用「King’s Ascend」。本條款說明我們在提供服務時處理資料的方式，請務必詳讀。

一、資料蒐集與使用
  • 本遊戲僅在裝置上儲存遊戲狀態（盤面、分數、最佳紀錄等），不會上傳至伺服器。
  • 我們不會蒐集可識別個人身份的資訊（如姓名、電子郵件、電話等）。
  • 您若透過電子郵件或社群聯絡我們，資料僅用於回覆問題與客服支援。
二、資料儲存
  • 所有遊戲資料皆存放於您的行動裝置本地儲存區域。
三、第三方服務
  • 本遊戲目前未整合任何第三方分析、廣告或雲端儲存服務。
  • 若未來新增第三方服務，我們將更新本條款並另行通知。
四、兒童隱私
  • 本遊戲為一般向產品，未特別設計給兒童使用。
  • 未成年人使用時，請監護人協助閱讀並同意本條款。
五、本條款之更新
  • 我們可能因應功能調整或法規變更而更新本條款，並於本頁公告更新日期。

若有任何疑問，歡迎聯絡 tyouxipindao@gmail.com。

King's Ascend Privacy Policy
Last Updated: November 8, 2025

Welcome to “King’s Ascend.” This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information while delivering the experience. Please review it carefully.

1. Data Collection & Use
  • The Game stores gameplay state (board layout, score, best record, etc.) locally on your device. No data is uploaded to our servers.
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • If you contact us by email or social media, we will use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry or support request.
2. Storage
  • All gameplay data remains inside your device’s local storage (SharedPreferences).
  • Removing or reinstalling the Game may erase this data because it is not synchronized elsewhere.
3. Third-Party Services
  • The Game does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or cloud storage providers.
  • If we ever add such services, this Policy will be updated and the changes will be communicated here.
4. Children’s Privacy
  • The Game is designed for a general audience and is not specifically directed at children.
  • Guardians should review this Policy with minors and ensure they understand the guidelines before playing.
5. Policy Updates
  • We may revise this Policy when features change or legal requirements evolve. Updates will be posted here along with a revised “Last Updated” date.

If you have any questions, please contact us at tyouxipindao@gmail.com.
